# Welcome to SGM

**Sales Governance Manager (SGM)** is an enterprise platform for managing sales compensation program governance. It provides a unified view across policy design, day-to-day operations, dispute resolution, and executive oversight.

## What SGM Does

SGM helps organizations manage the full lifecycle of sales compensation governance:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Policy Management" icon="file-contract">
    Create, maintain, and version 17+ Sales Compensation Policies (SCPs) covering all governance scenarios.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gap Analysis" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Upload compensation documents and automatically identify compliance gaps against policy requirements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Approval Workflows" icon="clipboard-check">
    Route decisions through SGCC and CRB committees with SLA tracking and audit trails.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-Powered Insights" icon="robot">
    Ask governance questions in natural language and get contextual answers backed by policy references.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Four Operational Modes

SGM organizes work into four distinct modes, each serving different user personas:

| Mode | Purpose | Primary Users |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Design** | Create policies, templates, and governance frameworks | Consultants, CoE, Policy Architects |
| **Operate** | Manage documents, approvals, and day-to-day operations | Compensation Team, Revenue Ops |
| **Dispute** | Handle exceptions, disputes, and case resolution | Sales Field, Case Workers |
| **Oversee** | Monitor compliance, committees, and governance health | Executives, Compliance Officers |

## Platform Highlights

- **26 policies** in the library (17 SCPs + custom policies)
- **929 Knowledge Base cards** across 8 SPM pillars
- **70+ document types** with version control
- **Multi-step approval workflows** with SLA tracking
- **AI assistants** for governance questions and insights
