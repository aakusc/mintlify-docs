## Financial Controls

### SCP-001: Clawback and Recovery Policy

**Category**: Financial Controls
**Framework Area**: Clawback/Recovery
**Legal Review Required**: Yes

Defines when and how commission payments can be recovered from sales personnel. Covers:
- Clawback triggers (deal cancellation, customer churn, fraud)
- Recovery periods and timelines
- Calculation methodology
- Employee notification requirements
- Hardship provisions

### SCP-008: Draws and Guarantees Policy

**Category**: Financial Controls
**Framework Area**: Draws/Guarantees

Establishes rules for draw payments and guaranteed compensation. Covers:
- Recoverable vs. non-recoverable draws
- Guarantee structures and eligibility
- Transition periods
- True-up calculations
- Forfeiture conditions

### SCP-011: Payment Timing Policy

**Category**: Payroll
**Framework Area**: Payment Timing

Defines when commission payments are processed. Covers:
- Payment schedules (monthly, quarterly)
- Earnings recognition
- Payment thresholds
- Hold provisions
- Adjustment timing

## Legal Compliance

### SCP-005: Section 409A Compliance Policy

**Category**: Legal Compliance
**Framework Area**: Compliance (409A, State Wage)
**Legal Review Required**: Yes

Ensures compensation plans comply with IRS Section 409A regulations on deferred compensation. Covers:
- Short-term deferral rules
- Payment timing requirements
- Plan document requirements
- Correction procedures
- Penalty provisions

### SCP-006: State Wage Law Compliance Policy

**Category**: Legal Compliance
**Framework Area**: Compliance (409A, State Wage)
**Legal Review Required**: Yes

Addresses state-specific wage and hour requirements. Covers:
- State-by-state requirements
- Commission payment timing
- Wage statement requirements
- Expense reimbursement
- Final pay rules by state

### SCP-017: International Requirements Policy

**Category**: Legal Compliance
**Framework Area**: International Requirements

Covers compensation governance for international employees. Covers:
- Country-specific requirements
- Currency considerations
- Tax implications
- Local labor laws
- Cross-border transfers

## HR Policies

### SCP-009: Leave of Absence Policy

**Category**: HR Policies
**Framework Area**: Leave of Absence

Defines compensation treatment during leaves. Covers:
- Quota adjustments during leave
- Commission treatment
- Protected vs. unprotected leave
- Return-to-work provisions
- FMLA considerations

### SCP-012: Termination and Final Pay Policy

**Category**: HR Policies
**Framework Area**: Termination/Final Pay

Establishes rules for final compensation upon termination. Covers:
- Voluntary vs. involuntary termination
- Commission on pending deals
- Pipeline treatment
- Clawback application
- State final pay requirements

### SCP-016: New Hire and Onboarding Policy

**Category**: HR Policies
**Framework Area**: New Hire/Onboarding

Defines compensation treatment for new employees. Covers:
- Ramping schedules
- Guarantee structures
- Quota ramping
- Territory assignment
- Training period treatment

## Using Policies

### In Gap Analysis

When you run a [gap analysis](/user-guide/governance-analysis), SGM checks your documents against requirements from these policies.

### In Plan Templates

[Plan templates](/user-guide/plan-management) reference policies to ensure plans address all governance requirements.

### In Approvals

[Approval workflows](/user-guide/approvals) reference policies when making governance decisions.

## Customization

While SCPs cannot be modified, you can:
- Create custom policies based on SCPs
- Add tenant-specific policies
- Extend SCP requirements for your organization

See the [Policy Library](/user-guide/policy-library) user guide for details.
